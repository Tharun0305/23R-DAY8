How to plan the day

Here are some tips for planning your day

SET GOALS

Define what you want to achieve and turn those goals into monthly action plans. You can use smart goals to help you track your progress and adjust your actions

Make a to-do list

 Write down your tasks for the day or week and rank them in order of importance. You can also your list between work and life and include some quick tasks

Use time blocking

 your day blocks of time and assign a task to each block This can help you visualize your day
 Stick to your plan
Try to turn off distractions and mono-task. You can also refer back to your list often
